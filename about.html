This game was created by me, Harrison Marley. A student of the University of Western Australia.

spacePerson was made as a project for the unit CITS3403 Agile Web Development.


It's a space themed game, aiming for the 8-bit aesthetic, perhaps a callback to a

simpler time in gaming. I like the combination of retro and minimalism in these games.



  • Sound effects were generated using the http://www.bfxr.net/ sound generator
  • Game outline greatly assisted by stackOverflow
